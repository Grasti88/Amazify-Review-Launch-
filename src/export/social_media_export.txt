ARL Social Media Export Engine

Purpose
Export Amazon product review videos to multiple platforms.

Supported Platforms
-------------------
YouTube (16:9, 2–10 mins)
TikTok (9:16, 15–60 secs)
Facebook (1:1 or 16:9, 30 secs–5 mins)
LinkedIn (1:1, professional tone)

Workflow
--------
1. Select finished review video
2. Choose platform
3. Apply platform-specific template
4. Insert affiliate link
5. Export video

Admin Options
-------------
- Enable/disable platforms
- Track export failures
- Log successful uploads
